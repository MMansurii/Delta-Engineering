PROJECT NO. NSP-WTP-2024-001

Electrical Building
Water Treatment Plant

Complete structural design and analysis of a 3-story reinforced concrete moment-resisting frame with integrated mat and isolated footing foundation system. Finite element analysis with soil-structure interaction, designed per ACI 318-19 and ASCE 7-22 for Seismic Design Category D.

COMPLETED STRUCTURE
SECTION 01

Project Scope

The Electrical Building serves as the main power distribution hub for the water treatment facility, housing 20/0.4 kV transformers, MV/LV switchgear, MCC panels, capacitor banks, and the centralized SCADA control room.

Structural system consists of a reinforced concrete intermediate moment-resisting frame (IMRF) with Response Modification Factor R=5. The lateral load-resisting system includes perimeter moment frames in both orthogonal directions with concrete shear walls at the stairwell core for additional torsional stiffness.

Foundation design integrates a 600mm thick mat foundation beneath the transformer bays (to distribute concentrated equipment loads and accommodate future replacement) with 1.2m Ã— 1.2m isolated spread footings at perimeter columns. Soil-structure interaction modeled using Winkler springs with subgrade modulus Ks = 3.5 kg/cmÂ³ based on plate load test results.

Challenges

Heavy concentrated loads from dual 1600 kVA transformers (8,500 kg each) required localized mat foundation design with punching shear verification. Strict vibration criteria for sensitive electrical equipment limited floor accelerations to 0.005g under seismic conditions. Coordination of 800mm deep cable trenches with foundation layout while maintaining structural integrity posed significant design constraints. Future equipment replacement access required removable roof sections with temporary shoring provisions. Aggressive exposure conditions demanded enhanced concrete durability and increased cover requirements.

Engineering Methodology

01

Full 3D finite element model with 4-node shell elements for slabs/walls (mesh size 0.5m) and 2-node frame elements for beams/columns. Rigid diaphragm assumption at each floor level. P-Delta effects included for gravity-lateral interaction.

02
