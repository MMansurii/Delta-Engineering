Our Services

Comprehensive structural engineering solutions across diverse sectors and specializations

Commercial Buildings

Complete structural analysis and design for office towers, retail centers, mixed-use developments, and commercial building complexes of all scales and heights.

  • High-rise office towers and business centers
  • Shopping malls and retail commercial spaces
  • Mixed-use commercial developments

Residential Buildings

Comprehensive structural engineering for single-family homes, multi-family housing, condominium towers, and residential high-rise building complexes.

  • Single-family and multi-family residential homes
  • Condominium towers and apartment buildings
  • High-rise residential structures

Industrial Facilities

Heavy-duty structural design for manufacturing plants, warehouse facilities, distribution centers, and industrial processing complexes.

  • Manufacturing and production plants
  • Warehouse and distribution center facilities
  • Heavy equipment and crane support structures

Bridge Design

Specialized bridge engineering including structural analysis, design, and construction management for all bridge types and spans.

  • Highway and urban bridges
  • Pedestrian and cycling bridges
  • Bridge rehabilitation and retrofitting

Precast Systems

Engineering design and detailing for precast concrete components, connections, and complete precast structural systems.

  • Precast concrete panels and members
  • Structural connection design
  • Parking structures and garages

Seismic Assessment

Comprehensive seismic evaluation, risk assessment, and retrofit design to ensure structural resilience against earthquakes.

  • Seismic vulnerability assessment
  • Retrofit and strengthening design
  • Performance-based design

About Us

Utilizing industry-leading expertise and adhering to international building codes

Engineering Excellence Since 2010

Delta Engineering is a leading structural engineering consulting firm with a strong track record of delivering high-quality projects across commercial, residential, industrial, and infrastructure sectors.

Our multidisciplinary team of licensed professional engineers brings expertise and innovation to every challenge, utilizing advanced software tools including ETABS, SAP2000, SAFE, and Revit for comprehensive BIM integration.

International Building Codes

Full compliance with major building codes including IBC (International Building Code), NBCC (National Building Code of Canada), ASCE 7, ACI 318, AISC, Eurocode, and AS/NZS (Australian/New Zealand Standards).

  • Licensed Professional Engineers
  • BIM & Advanced Software Integration
  • International Code Compliance
  • 24/7 Project Support

Comprehensive Analysis Methods

Advanced finite element analysis, nonlinear analysis, seismic performance evaluation, wind load analysis, dynamic analysis, pushover analysis, and performance-based design methodologies for all structural systems.

We specialize in full-scope services from feasibility studies to permit approvals and construction-phase support, tailored to meet the complex needs of our diverse clients.
